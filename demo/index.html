Flama

Flama is a geometric sans-serif typeface designed by Mario Feliciano and released through Village in 2006. The design features oval shapes similar to DIN but also has a touch more of a grotesque feel. Flama is available in 10 weights with matching italics, so it’s a flexible family suitable for a wide range of uses.
